Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bobbin by TutorCruncher?

Bobbin is an intelligent lesson management platform that helps tutoring businesses and educators to track lessons, monitor student progress, and generate actionable insights, all powered by AI.

Do I need to be an existing TutorCruncher customer to use Bobbin?

At the moment, Bobbin is only available for TutorCruncher customers. However, it will soon be available as a standalone platform. Register for the early access scheme to stay up to date.

How does AI work in Bobbin?

Bobbin uses AI to power its lesson planning and reporting tools. For lesson planning, it uses your prompts to create structured, curriculum-aligned lesson plans tailored to your students, ready in seconds and fully editable. After each session, it transcribes the lesson and automatically generates structured insights covering topics discussed, student engagement, and areas for improvement, so there's no manual note-taking and parents and administrators have a clear picture of every session.

Does Bobbin have a whiteboard for online learning?

Yes, Bobbin has an integrated virtual classroom using the Lessonspace online whiteboard, so your lessons are recorded and analysed in one platform without any extra setup.

The current early access version is focused on online lessons, recording and transcribing sessions to make reporting quicker, more accurate, and simpler, with support for in-person lessons coming in the months ahead.

How quickly can I get access to Bobbin?

If you're an existing TutorCruncher customer, you can sign up and get access to the platform today. For new customers, we'll be releasing a standalone platform in the coming months. Sign up for the waitlist to stay in the loop.
